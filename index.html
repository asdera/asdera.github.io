Games

Scorpion

Power your inner Scorpion to shoot balls at other balls in this Retro-style shooter. This is a fast-paced survival game accompanied by sweet synthwave music. This is a submission to Github Game Off 2017. PRESS G IF YOU ARE EXPERIENCING LAG

Bhoppers

Bhoppers is a 2D platformer browser game perfect for anyone wanting to relax, meet new people, or just have some fun! Bhoppers is special in its simplicity yet extremely challenging difficulty.

Bottle Flipper

A fun sandbox game created with matter-js. Try to flip the water-bottle onto the target. To do this, just drag the water-bottle in any direction. Play around with basketballs, huge boxes and more. Try clicking the screen and using the WASD keys.

Neon Lands

A Completely Bizarre Unity Shooter Game. In a Neon world inhabited only by shapes, defeat your 2-D enemies and collect 3-D powerups. Eliminate the evil Octagon wizard and thwart his plans of squishing the universe flat.

Acers

An explosive two-player tank jam. Shoot shells packed with gunpowder across the field in real time. Duel your to determine which plyer is more skilled. Use Arrow keys and WASD keys to move. Hold and release Spacebar and Enter to shoot.
